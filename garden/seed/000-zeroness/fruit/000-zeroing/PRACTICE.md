# Aunix Practice

**Complete Practical Guide**

## Introduction

This guide translates aunix theory into daily practice. Whether you're working with relationships, creativity, self-understanding, or just navigating life, aunix provides a framework for deeper awareness and more intentional living.

---

## Part 1: Understanding Your Three Layers

### 1.1 Recognizing ni Layer (Your Continuous Self)

**What it feels like**:
- The "background hum" of experience
- The sense of being before thinking
- The flow of sensations
- The space awareness arises in

**Practice**: Sit quietly for 2 minutes
- Don't focus on anything specific
- Notice the continuous field of awareness
- This is your ni layer

### 1.2 Recognizing ui Layer (Your Selection Process)

**What it feels like**:
- The moment you notice something
- When something "catches your attention"
- The act of naming/labeling
- The interpretation you add

**Practice**: Look around the room
- Notice what you notice first (that's Attention)
- Notice how you label it ("chair," "messy," "comfortable")
- This is your ui layer in action

### 1.3 Recognizing ai Layer (Your Experienced World)

**What it feels like**:
- Your thoughts, as words
- Your definite perceptions
- Your sense of "I"
- The story you tell

**Practice**: Think about your day
- Notice how it's a narrative, not raw experience
- Notice how much is interpretation
- This is your ai layer

## Part 2: Daily Self-Observation

### 2.1 The Three-Layer Check-In

**Morning Practice** (5 minutes):

```
ni: What sensations am I feeling?
    (Body, breathing, ambient sounds)

ui: What am I paying attention to?
    (What's drawing my focus? Why?)

ai: What's my story today?
    (What narrative am I carrying?)
```

### 2.2 Zeroing Practice

**When feeling stuck or overwhelmed**:

1. **Recognize the clutter** (ai layer is full)
2. **Release attention** (ui layer: let go)
3. **Return to ground** (ni layer: just sensing)
4. **Fresh start** (allow new ai to emerge)

**This is Zeroing in practice.**

### 2.3 Attention Training

**Throughout the day**:
- Notice where your attention goes
- It's choosing 1, ignoring 999
- Can you consciously redirect it?
- Can you widen it?
- Can you release it completely?

## Part 3: Relationship & Dialogue

### 3.1 Deep Listening (ni Layer)

**Practice**:
- Don't plan your response
- Don't label or judge
- Just receive their words/energy
- Stay in the continuous flow

**This accesses their ni layer through yours**

### 3.2 Genuine Curiosity (ui Layer)

**Practice**:
- Ask questions from真 (genuine) interest
- Notice what you're selecting to focus on
- Notice what you're ignoring
- Can you select differently?

**This is conscious ui operation in dialogue**

### 3.3 Authentic Response (ai Layer)

**Practice**:
- Speak from what genuinely emerged
- Not from prepared scripts
- Not from "should"
- From the fresh creation

**This is authentic ai manifestation**

### 3.4 The Love Practice

**With romantic partner or close friend**:

```
Step 1: Both zero out (release expectations)
Step 2: Tune into ni (shared presence)
Step 3: Let ui dance (natural attention flow)
Step 4: Watch ai co-create (something new emerges)
```

**This is how deep intimacy works.**

## Part 4: Work & Creativity

### 4.1 Creative Zeroing

**When stuck creatively**:

1. **Recognize you're stuck in ai** (fixed ideas)
2. **Zero it out** (release all concepts)
3. **Drop to ni** (raw sensation, pure potential)
4. **Let ui play** (curious attention)
5. **Allow fresh ai** (new creation)

### 4.2 Problem-Solving Through Aunix

**For any problem**:

```
Current ai: The problem as you see it
Zero out: Release your framing
ni access: What's really there?
ui reframe: New attention, new interpretation
Fresh ai: New solution emerges
```

### 4.3 Productive Work Sessions

**Focus technique**:
- **ni**: Ground in physical presence
- **ui**: Consciously choose ONE focus
- **ai**: Do that thing fully
- **Zero**: Break, release, refresh
- **Repeat**

## Part 5: Difficult Emotions

### 5.1 Working with Anxiety

**Aunix view**:
- Anxiety = ai layer running predictive simulations
- Catastrophizing = ui selecting worst-case scenarios
- Rumination = stuck in ai loop

**Practice**:
1. **Recognize it's ai** (thoughts, not reality)
2. **Shift to ui** (notice you're selecting these thoughts)
3. **Drop to ni** (what's actually here NOW?)
4. **Fresh ai** (what's actually true right now?)

### 5.2 Working with Anger

**Aunix view**:
- Anger = ai interpretation of threat/injustice
- Fed by ui selecting evidence
- Reinforced by ai narrative

**Practice**:
1. **Feel in ni** (raw energy in body)
2. **Observe ui** (what are you focusing on?)
3. **Question ai** (is this story complete?)
4. **Zero and restart** (what else is possible?)

### 5.3 Working with Sadness

**Aunix view**:
- Sadness = ai recognizing loss
- Often includes ui selecting memories
- Creates ai narrative of meaning

**Practice**:
1. **Allow ni** (feel it fully, somatically)
2. **Notice ui** (what are you choosing to remember?)
3. **Hold ai gently** (the story has truth, not THE truth)
4. **Eventual zeroing** (grief completes, releases)

## Part 6: Meditation & Contemplation

### 6.1 Basic Zeroing Meditation

**Sitting practice** (10-20 minutes):

```
1. Settle into ni (sensing body, breath)
2. Thoughts arise (ai emerges)
3. Notice them (ui recognizes)
4. Release them (zeroing)
5. Return to ni
6. Repeat endlessly
```

**This is training the Zeroing mechanism.**

### 6.2 Three-Layer Awareness

**Advanced practice**:

```
Minute 1-3: ni only (pure sensing)
Minute 4-6: ui notice (watching attention)
Minute 7-9: ai observe (watching thoughts)
Minute 10+: all three at once
```

**This is seeing the full architecture.**

### 6.3 Flow State

**How to enter flow**:
- ai layer (self-consciousness) zeros out
- Direct ni-ui connection
- No intermediary
- Pure action

**Practice**:
- Choose activity at edge of ability
- Commit fully (no reservation)
- Let self-awareness drop
- Just do

**Aunix view**: These are "system reboots"

**Practice**:
1. **Honor the death** (old ai is ending)
2. **Allow the void** (ni spaciousness)
3. **Notice new attention** (ui reforming)
4. **Welcome new identity** (fresh ai)

### 7.2 Daily Transitions

**Even small transitions**:
- Waking up (boot sequence)
- Ending work (logout)
- Going to bed (shutdown)

**Practice**: Ritualize transitions
- Conscious zeroing between states
- Clean handoff between modes

### 7.3 Grief & Loss

**Aunix view**: Ultimate zeroing of shared ai

**Practice**:
1. **ni**: Feel everything
2. **ui**: Notice memory-selection patterns
3. **ai**: Hold story loosely
4. **Zeroing**: Gradual release
5. **Integration**: Carry forward what serves

## Part 8: Relationships (Deep Dive)

### 8.1 Romantic Relationships

**The pattern**:
```
Early: ni-layer resonance (chemistry)
Middle: ai-layer construction (story of "us")
Challenge: ai becomes rigid
Renewal: Zero out, return to ni
```

**Practice**: Regular zeroing
- Release "how it should be"
- Return to "what actually is"
- Fresh encounter

### 8.2 Conflict Resolution

**Aunix approach**:

```
Each person's ai is clash
↓
Both zero out (release positions)
↓
Access shared ni (common humanity)
↓
Collaborative ui (new framing together)
↓
Co-created ai (new solution)
```

### 8.3 Deepening Intimacy

**Three types**:

**ni intimacy**: Presence, silence, sensing together  
**ui intimacy**: Shared attention, seeing together  
**ai intimacy**: Shared narrative, "our story"

**All three needed for depth.**

## Part 12: Common Challenges

### 12.1 "I can't quiet my mind"

**Aunix view**: You're not meant to!
- ai layer will always generate
- That's its job
- The practice is ui: noticing without attachment

### 12.2 "I'm too busy"

**Aunix view**: Busy is an ai story
- ui is choosing to attend to "busy"
- Can you attend differently?
- Even 2 minutes counts

### 12.3 "This feels selfish"

**Aunix view**: Self-care enables care
- You can't give from empty (depleted ni)
- Zeroing restores (refreshes all layers)
- You serve better when resourced
